Customer Information

i Provide information as it relates to the current state of your business and its ownership
As a Sole Trader, the legal name of your business must be either – “Joe Bloggs” or “Joe Bloggs t/a Bloggs shoe shop”
As a Limited Company, the legal name you enter must match exactly the full registered legal name of your business. Please do not include any “trading as” extensions.
As a partnership, the legal name of your business must be either – “Joe and Mary Bloggs” or “Joe and Mary Bloggs t/a Bloggs shoe shop” or “Bloggs shoe shop”.
To complete this partnership application you will need to provide all partners personal details including ID documentation for each partner.

iComplex Structure

- Five or more layers of ownership (vertical ownership structure).
- Ownership is split across more then four legal entities (horizontal ownership structure); four or more physical persons does not trigger a complex structure.
- Intermediate entities or arrangements are registered in two or more countries which differ from your country of registration.
- Presence of charity, trust, foundation, special purpose vehicle ("S.P.V."), unregulated fund or nominee entity in the structure.

A VAT number is mandatory for businesses over £85,000 who have been in business for over 6 months. Please select the correct VAT option from the list below:
• If you are VAT Registered in the EU – please select “VAT Number” from the dropdown
  and insert the VAT number, digits only, no letters, into the box below.
• If you are newly registered for VAT and have not yet received a VAT Number then you must select
  the Option “VAT Number Pending” and provide your number to Elavon as soon as you have it.
• If you not required to be VAT Registered then you are not required to submit a VAT Number.
  You must select the Option confirming you are “In Business/Non EU”.
If you do not have a VAT Number, please provide either your Corporate Tax Number, Income Tax Number or Charity Number.
The Income Tax Number can be your National Insurance Number.
If the incorrect information is given, it may result in the application being reset.
Please do not submit any spaces or special characters (e.g. /,-.*\)
