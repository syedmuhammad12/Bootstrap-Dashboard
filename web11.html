Document Upload

i Please select each type of document below and upload or use eDocs mobile app ( iOS / Android ) and scan QR code below


 

Proof of ID
(authorised signatory)

- Unexpired passport
- Unexpired UK driving license (full or provisional)
- EEA or national Identity card
- A Northern Ireland Electoral Office Identity Card
- Modern Firearms certificate



 

Proof of Business Address


- Utility Bill - no older than 6 months old and should show your company name and trading address
- Signed Lease Agreement – which should show your company name and trading address
- Acquiring Statement - No older than 6 months old and should show your company name and trading address
- Site Visit form - completed by Sales Agent

 

Proof of Banking
 

- A voided cheque [note that cover of cheque book is not acceptable]
- A letter from the your bank (on bank letterhead paper)
- A print screen from the your bank with visible bank domain or padlock
- A bank statement (copy or online statement with visible bank logo)
- A processing statement
- An email from the your bank with visible bank domain that can be verified on bank's website
- A bank Giro

As an established business
(greater than 3 months old) please:

- Obtain last 3 months business bank statements if business greater than 3 months old
- If a sole trader without a business bank account, provide last 3 months most recent personal bank account statements
